Personal

Last year a litter of kittens were born in my back yard that all struggled with health and needed to be rescued. After five weeks of antibiotics and several vet trips, they seemed to be doing okay! Due to the constant care of these kittens, I got so attached that I kept three of them and the mom. This is the story how I went from a dog person, to an owner of three cats.


Work

After graduating from Ohio State in 2016 I've moved around a lot. I started my first engineering job in 2017 as a Laser Welding Engineer / Supplier Quality Engineer in Cleveland, OH. This provided great experience and a meaniful work environment. Though I enjoyed this job, for career growth, I took a job at Honda in Indiana. Here I was one of two Weldability Engineers responsable for the 250 resistance welding robots in the welding department. This was another satifying job, but in 2020 I moved back to Ohio for family reasons. I'm not living in Columbus, OH, and drive to Troy for work daily as an Arc Welding Engineer. This is another great opportunity that I am greatful for. Here I got to spend more time programming robots, which solidified my interest in Computer Programming!
